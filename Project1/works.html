The Musings of a College Student - Curtis Duvall

When I go back and think about my college experience, I realise that I missed out on a lot of crazy stuff because I chose the stem field.When I first went to college, I was told how much fun that I would have in college. I haven't been to any parties which is a good thing since it has kept me out of trouble. When I think back, I can see all the connections that I made and all of the friends that I have run into. It makes me happy now that I am about to graduate that I went to college and chose the engineering major.
